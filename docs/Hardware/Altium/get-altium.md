Altium Designer is a powerful, industry-leading PCB (Printed Circuit Board) design software used by the Electronics team at RIT Racing to create and develop complex circuit boards for the car’s electrical systems.

---

To get a free student license and download Altium, follow these steps:

1. Follow this link: <https://education.altium.com/>

2. Click **Enroll for Free** and sign up with your school email (e.g., abc1234@rit.edu — do not use @g.rit.edu) and input your college's name. Afterwords press start verification.

    <div style="text-align: center; margin-top: 30px;">
        <img src="../Images/enroll.png" alt="Altium Enroll"  style="max-width: 70%; height: auto;"/>
    </div>

3. Once approached with the image below. Type in the college's name. Ocassionally Rochester Institute of Technology does not autofill, but input it anyways and continue. Then input your school email (not @g.rit.edu) and get the verification code.
    <div style="text-align: center; margin-top: 30px;">
        <img src="/../../Hardware/Altium/Images/altium-verification.png" alt="Altium Verification"  style="max-width: 70%; height: auto;"/>
    </div>
4. Click the link in the verification email and enter your verification code and school's email. After entering it will take you to the free enrollment page where you can enter in your information. Two email containing the Altium Student License and the Student License Verification link will be sent. These emails usually takes a couple of minutes to send so be patient.
<br>

5. Verify your Altium student license email
    <div style="text-align: center; margin-top: 30px;">
        <img src="/../../Hardware/Altium/Images/altium-verify-student-email.png" alt="Altium Verify Student Email"  style="max-width: 70%; height: auto;"/>
    </div>

6. Activate Altium Live

    <div style="text-align: center; margin-top: 30px;">
        <img src="/../../Hardware/Altium/Images/altium-email.png" alt="Altium Email"  style="max-width: 60%; height: auto;"/>
    </div>


7.  Download the installer from the activate Altium Live page, alternatively from the Altium Downloads link from the previous email you can get the latest version of Altium Designer:
    <div style="text-align: center; margin-top: 30px;">
        <img src="/../../Hardware/Altium/Images/activate-altium.png" alt="Activate Altium"  style="max-width: 60%; height: auto;"/>
    </div>

    <div style="text-align: center; margin-top: 30px;">
        <img src="/../../Hardware/Altium/Images/downloads-page.png" alt="Downloads Page"  style="max-width: 90%; height: auto;"/>
    </div>


8.  After downloading, open the setup.exe file and follow the installation steps. You will be asked to sign in and select features. Check all the boxes to ensure full functionality:

    <div style="text-align: center; margin-top: 30px;">
        <img src="/../../Hardware/Altium/Images/installation-functionality.png" alt="Altium Installation Functionality"  style="max-width: 80%; height: auto;"/>
    </div>

9.  Choose a location in your file system to install the software and documents. You can leave it as the default location.

10. Once installation is complete, sign in to your Altium account.

    <div style="text-align: center; margin-top: 30px;">
        <img src="/../../Hardware/Altium/Images/signin.png" alt="Altium Sign-In"  style="max-width: 100%; height: auto;"/>
    </div>

11. Activate your license.

    <div style="text-align: center; margin-top: 30px;">
        <img src="/../../Hardware/Altium/Images/use-license.png" alt="Altium Use License"  style="max-width: 100%; height: auto;"/>
    </div>

You now have Altium Designer installed and ready to use with your student license. If you ever need to renew your license the same process should be followed.
